INTRODUCTION

An Overall Introduction to Luoyang

View

Located in the northwest of Henan Province, Luoyang borders the Qinling Mountains in the West and the plain in the east. It has beautiful scenery.

History

Luoyang has a long history, with more than 3000 years of historical accumulation. Thirteen dynasties successively established their capital here.

Personage

In the course of history, many famous figures emerged, such as Cao Cao, Emperor Wei Wu, Liu Xiu, Emperor Guangwu and Emperor Wu Zetian.
